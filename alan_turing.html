Alan Turing (1912–1954)

Alan Turing was a mathematician born on June 23, 1912 in Maida Vale, London, United Kingdom. He died on June 7, 1954 in Wilmslow, United Kingdom. He was a mathematician, code breaker, and one of the founders of early digital computers and modern computing as we know it today. He was also one of the major innovators in Enigma code breaking in collaboration with the Poles.
