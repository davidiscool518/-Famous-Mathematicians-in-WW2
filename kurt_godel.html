Kurt Gödel (1906–1978) Personal Life and atribuites

Kurt Gödel was an important figure of modern mathematics, particularly in the early to mid 20th century. He was born in 1906 in Brno, Czechoslovakia and died in 1978 in Princeton, New Jersey. He was of Austro-Hungarian origin and was a native German speaker at home and later in life.
